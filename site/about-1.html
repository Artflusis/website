Our Story

Carmel Organics is an organic & herbal ingredients manufacture and distribution company. Started in 2012, Carmel Organics has grown into a team of 15 permanent members. Situated in Neemuch, a small town in Madhya Pradesh, we have been able to supply our products into developed markets in 5 continents. We believe in producing world class products and test them using International standards, it is due to this we have gained trust of one of the India's largest herbal product manufacturing company.
The company has grown significantly over the last few years. 500 Certified Farmers have been registered till date.Over 1500 ha of land is being used for the production of organic products. A total of 240 different products are being supplied to various part of country and abroad. The next step is to set up local offices in countries like US, UK, Australia. Another important thing we have in mind is to use technology to ease operations at each step, and to check whether our products are being delivered without any loss in quality.

Vision

Our vision is to become the largest E2E & B2B Herb company in the world. and our every step is intended to make this vision a reality.Our key focus areas for the coming 5 years are to develop a wast network of producers of Medicinal herbs , to develop a world class infrastructure to process , pack and handle the herbs & to go to front end distribution channels across the globe.
