The Last of Those Days

Short Film - The Last of Those Days

‘The Last of Those Days’ is a remembrance of the last day in a hostel peopled with authority figures and harassers. It tries to capture the isolation felt by a nine years old kid, newly caged in the place, and the psychological motivations behind his escape. The drama – the little that there is – unfolds on August 14 when the boy finds his freedom at midnight.
